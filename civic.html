    Clean Walk in Toulouse

    At the end of our first week at ENSEEIHT, everyone took part in a clean-up walk. It was a fun and engaging moment, where we came together to clean the streets of Toulouse. Not only did we contribute to cleaning the area, but it also helped raise awareness about environmental issues within our community.

    Collective workshop : "Fresque du Climat" with 8 classmates.

    This collaborative exercise was an opportunity to dive into climate issues, analyze their causes and consequences, and discuss potential solutions. It encouraged critical thinking and collective responsibilits. This led to the creation of a tree-like poster illustrating the causal links between various aspects of climate change. We then had a discussion session with our facilitator, where we explored potential solutions that could help drive change.

    Delegate of my lab-lessons-group at ENSEEIHT

    As the delegate of my TP (practical work) group, I play a crucial role in facilitating communication between the professors and the students in my group. I ensure that everyone is informed about course requirements, assignments, and any changes in the schedule. By acting as a liaison, I help ensure that all students feel supported and that their concerns are conveyed to the professors in a timely manner. This role has allowed me to develop leadership and organizational skills while also fostering a sense of responsibility toward my peers.

    Creating a Learning Hub During the COVID-19 Pandemic

    During the COVID-19 pandemic in 2020, while in high school, I took the initiative with a classmate to create a Discord server to maintain the continuity of our education. The server became a virtual classroom, where we could share resources, host study sessions, and keep in touch. It was essential to organize the server to make it user-friendly, as it was a space not only for academic work but also for social interaction. We also provided training for teachers on how to use the platform, ensuring that they could effectively engage with students. The project was an important way to ensure that our peers could continue learning and collaborating, despite the circumstances.

    Tutoring in Mathematics and Physics-Chemistry

    I provided tutoring in mathematics and physics-chemistry to middle and high school students. This experience allowed me to share my knowledge and help students better understand concepts they found difficult. I tailored my lessons to meet the specific needs of each student, helping them build confidence and improve their academic performance. It also helped me develop my communication skills, as I had to explain complex ideas in a clear and accessible way. It was truly rewarding to see my students progress and succeed, knowing I had contributed to their educational journey.
